Zuppa Toscana

Description

Does the cold weather make you want to eat something warm?, Then this zuppa toscana is the great way to fulfill that!, By the amount of spice that make you want to eat more of this!, This recipe is really delicious!.

Ingredients

  • Broth

  • Meat

  • Spices

  • Vegetables

  • Cream

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage with the red pepper flakes.

  2. Cook the bacon, then cook the onion and garlin in the bacon drippings.

  3. Add the broth and bring to a boil, then add the potatoes and simmer.

  4. Reduce the heat and stir in the csream, cooked sausage, and spinach.
